| Competitor | Positioning |
|---|---|
| **Cleantech** | Local Egyptian brand, price advantage |
| **Egypt Car Care** | Local brand, local market knowledge |
| **Independent detailing centers** | Service-based, often using imported products |

### 6.3 Key Competitive Dynamics
1. **Price sensitivity** is the #1 challenge ‚Äî Egyptian consumers are value-conscious
2. **Premium brands** (Meguiar's, Sonax, Menzerna) compete on quality and enthusiast loyalty
3. **Mass brands** (Turtle Wax, ABRO) compete on availability and price
4. **3M's dual role** (parent of Meguiar's + own car care line) creates an interesting market dynamic
5. **E-commerce platforms** (Ubuy, Amazon) are already selling Meguiar's products ‚Äî potential channel conflict with official distributor
