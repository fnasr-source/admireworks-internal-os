The "Launchpad" Sprint

From idea to revenue-generating brand: a 12-week sprint to build the "Lighting Stylist" platform and secure the first Saudi B2B leads.

The Problem

  • Decision Paralysis: Buyers face 460k+ options online and freeze without expert guidance.
  • Trust Gap: High anxiety about fragile shipping and "wrong fit" returns (15-20% industry rates).
  • Missing Middle: Market is split between cheap marketplaces (enarat.com) and expensive showrooms.

Our Solution

  • The "Decision Engine": A quiz-led platform that curates "3 Perfect Matches" instead of 10,000 options.
  • Risk Removal: "See it in your room" AR visualization + "Safe Arrival Guarantee" insurance.
  • Hybrid Strategy: B2C cash flow engine + B2B "Projects KSA" export arm for Saudi mega-projects.

What We Will Deliver

  • Brand Identity & Positioning: A trust-first brand that signals "Expert Stylist," not just "Retailer."
  • High-Convert Website: Fast, mobile-first store with specific "Room Style Quiz" funnel.
  • Room Visualizer Tool: AR feature allowing customers to preview lights in their actual space to reduce returns.
  • Content Production: 5 high-quality "Review" videos and 30-day social content strategy.
  • Supplier Network: Vetted dropship partnerships with 3+ top-tier suppliers (zero inventory risk).
  • Acquisition System: Launched Meta/Google ad campaigns optimized for Egypt's low CPM ($0.59).
  • B2B Pipeline Asset: Dedicated "Projects KSA" landing page targeting Saudi hotel developers.
  • Operations Setup: CRM, WhatsApp API, and payment/installment (Sympl/ValU) integrations live.

  • Growth: Egypt LED market growing to $650M by 2033 (CAGR 3.65%).
  • Ad Advantage: Meta CPM in Egypt is $0.59 (17x cheaper than US/Global).
  • Saudi Boom: Vision 2030 investing $110B in 310k+ new hotel rooms.
  • Shift: "Mousky" model is failing modern, time-poor buyers who value convenience over haggling.
  • Trend: Trust and curation are the new currency, replacing raw catalogue size.

  • Homzmart: Mass market, low trust for fragile items.
  • Opportunity: Occupy the "Stylist" gap between mass marketplace and luxury showroom.

C) KSA Opportunity

  • Target: Architecture firms (Foster+Partners, Aedas) & Developers (NEOM, Red Sea).
  • Value Prop: "Egyptian Craftsmanship, Saudi Specs, Half the Price."
  • Strategy: Digital B2B outreach + Landing Page (no retail footprint needed yet).
